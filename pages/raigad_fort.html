Raigad Fort: A Symbol of Maratha Glory

  1. Entering The Fort

The Chit Darwaja is located at the foothills of the fort which is near the village of Pachad. From here the visitors start on their foot journey to the Khoob Ladha Buruj. And then they reach the main entrance to the fort, the Maha Darwaja. The massive entrance to the fort reflects the glory of the Marathas. There are ropeway facilities that you can use to directly go to the top of the fort if you do not want to climb 1737 steps. It will take you to the top of the fort directly near Mena Darwaja.
