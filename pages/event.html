Events Of Shivneri
Shivneri Fort,the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, hosts several significant events throughout the year that celebrate its historical and cultural importance. One of the most notable events is Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti on February 19, which marks the birth anniversary of the Maratha ruler. This event includes tributes, speeches, processions, and cultural performances that honor Shivaji's legacy. Another important occasion is Independence Day (August 15), when the fort becomes a site for patriotic celebrations, flag hoisting, and local performances. Additionally, trekking events and heritage walks are organized to explore the fort’s historical significance. Cultural performances such as Lavani and Lezim dance, as well as Maratha war reenactments, often take place during major festivals, bringing the fort’s rich history to life.

Collaborative Activities

Shivneri Fort Trekking

Embark on a thrilling trek to Shivneri Fort, exploring its gates, temples, and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Shivneri Marathon

Take part in the Shivneri Marathon, running around the fort and nearby areas, symbolizing the courage and legacy of Shivaji Maharaj.

Adventure Activities

Shivneri Fort is a popular trekking spot for adventure enthusiasts. Trekking events are often held where participants explore the fort and surrounding areas.

Historical Walks

Join a guided walk to learn about the rich history of Shivneri Fort, the birthplace of Shivaji Maharaj, and discover its significance in Maratha history.

Temple tour

A temple tour typically refers to visiting multiple temples in a specific region, often as part of a spiritual or cultural journey.

Shiv Jayanti Celebrations

Celebrate the birth anniversary of Shivaji Maharaj with special ceremonies, cultural performances, and patriotic activities at Shivneri Fort.

Shivneri Art Contest

Painting Competition

This competition invites all artists to showcase their creativity through paintings. Participants can express their vision, creativity, and mastery of various painting techniques, whether it be traditional, contemporary, or abstract art. The competition encourages diversity in styles, colors, and mediums.

Photography Competition

A competition for photographers to showcase their talent and passion for capturing stunning moments. Whether it's landscape, portrait, wildlife, or street photography, the goal is to capture compelling, thought-provoking images that tell a story or evoke emotion.

Powada Competition

Powada is a traditional Marathi form of poetry and performance, which includes both narration and music. The Powada Competition invites performers to showcase their skills in this vibrant art form, paying tribute to the historical and cultural significance of Maratha warriors and legends.
